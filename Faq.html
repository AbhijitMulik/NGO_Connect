FAQs

⁠ ⁠What services does NGO CONNECT provide?

▼

NGO CONNECT is a platform that helps users find NGOs categorized by their specific areas of work, such as NGOs for children, the elderly, and the disabled. We also provide a booking system for food donations, allowing you to schedule donations for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks.

How do I book a slot for food donation?

▼

To book a slot for food donation, simply visit our "Slot" section in Navbar ,select type of NGO , Select NGO Name , Select the Time Slot (breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks) ,Choose a Donation Date , If available , you’ll receive a confirmation else choose other Date. A final confirmaton Mail will be sent only if the Affirmation Form is submitted. !

⁠ ⁠Can I donate to multiple NGOs at once?

▼

Yes, you can. NGO CONNECT allows you to donate to multiple NGOs by selecting different organizations from the list during the slot booking process. You can choose a different NGO for each meal or a single NGO for all meals.

How do I find the right NGO for my cause?

▼

Our platform categorizes NGOs based specific types—children, elderly, disabled, etc. You can check Specific Type of NGO's for Further Details. .

What types of food donations are accepted?

▼

Generally, you can donate pre-cooked meals or packaged items for breakfast, lunch, dinner, or snacks. Make sure the Food is Hygienic and preferably Nutritious.

NGOCONNECT bridges donors and NGOs in Mumbai, enabling hands-on engagement and fostering impactful connections for positive change.
